About us

One kitchen. Honest flavor.

99 TopMeal is a family-owned Chinese noodle shop in the heart of Helsinki. Our chef brings the classics of his home region, Nanchang — slow-braised meat broths, fresh cold noodles, and the kind of seasoned sides you'd find at a kitchen table back home. The menu changes weekly with the season and what's fresh. Every main comes with six complimentary small plates — the way we've always done it.
