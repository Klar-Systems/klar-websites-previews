Our story

Syrian food.
Helsinki kitchen.

Tawook Lab brings the street food of Syria to central Helsinki — honest, flavour-forward dishes built around the tawook, the mezze table, and the falafel.

Head chef Kasem Al-Hallak has spent over two decades in professional kitchens. At Tawook Lab, that experience goes into every plate.

Around half our menu is vegetarian or vegan — the falafel and mezze plates work equally well as a full meal on their own.

What we make

The menu

Every dish is made from scratch. The menu rotates, so check our Instagram for the current selection.

01

Smoked Tawook

Our signature — slow-marinated chicken, rice, tahini, pomegranate molasses, toasted nuts. The dish Tawook Lab is built around.

02

Falafel

House-made from a daily-ground mix. Served with fresh accompaniments. A full meal on its own — popular with both vegetarians and meat-eaters.

03

Mezze

Hummus, baba ganoush, seasonal accompaniments. Best enjoyed shared — order as a starter or build a full mezze meal.

04

Syrian Meatballs

Spiced ground meat, slow-cooked, served with rice and sauce. One of the most ordered dishes on the main menu.

The full menu — including daily specials and seasonal dishes — is posted on @tawooklab. We also deliver via Wolt.
