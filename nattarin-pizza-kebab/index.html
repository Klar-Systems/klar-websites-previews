Our story

Fresh
Mediterranean.

Nattarin Pizza Kebab brings the flavors of the Mediterranean to Kangasala — freshly made pizzas, grilled kebabs, burgers, and authentic Mediterranean dishes.

Led by owner Mohammed Ali Akbari, every dish is prepared with care and quality ingredients. Whether you're looking for a quick bite or a full meal, we deliver late into the night.

We serve across multiple cuisines: family-sized pizzas, slow-grilled kebabs, chicken dishes, crispy falafel, fresh salads, and Mediterranean specialties. Available on Wolt and Foodora for delivery.
