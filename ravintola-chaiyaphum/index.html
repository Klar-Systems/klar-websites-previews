About us

Thai cuisine from Chaiyaphum

Ravintola Chaiyaphum is a sit-down Thai restaurant founded in 2014 by Saisamorn Mehtälä. Named after the Chaiyaphum province in central Thailand — owner Saisamorn's hometown — we bring authentic Thai flavors and hospitality to Raahe. Open Monday through Saturday with fresh ingredients, traditional recipes, and warm service.
