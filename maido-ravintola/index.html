About

A new chapter on Kauppakatu

MAIDO Sushi & Wok opened in December 2024 in the heart of Kuopio, taking over the well-known address at Kauppakatu 29 — home to a beloved Asian restaurant for over 15 years.

Under new ownership and with a fully renovated interior, MAIDO brings a fresh take on the Asian buffet: a spread of sushi, wok dishes, dim sum, and teppanyaki that's prepared in the kitchen and served straight to your table.
